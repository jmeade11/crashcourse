I enjoy life as a developer

Becoming a developer has changed my life. I love what I do and feel fulfilled everyday. That hadn't always been the case, so I decided to change careers a year ago. I enrolled at General Assembly in Boston and learned to code. Today, I'm employed full time as a front end developer with an awesome local startup.

I love being creative

The main reason I love being a developer is the creativity involved in building new things. Being a developer is challenging and sometimes hard, but I work with great people who have helped me grow my skills and given me the latitude to explore a lot of new areas in design and development.
